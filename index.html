The story hiding in plain sight.

The Pay-It-Forward slice program isn't just a nice gesture — it's a Portland story waiting to be told. When high school kids can walk in and eat, that ripples through families, teachers, and neighbors.

🍕 What if customers could see it in real time?

A simple display — "12 slices donated this week. 9 redeemed." — turns a quiet act of kindness into something the whole community participates in. People tip more. People come back. People tell their friends.
